Alan Riddell is a senior partner at Soloway Wright LLP in Ottawa, specializing in Employment, Labour, and Public Law. With over 25 years of experience, he advises and represents clients in complex workplace, human rights, constitutional, and administrative law matters, and has appeared before all levels of court, including the Supreme Court of Canada. Fluent in English and French, Alan is known for his strategic, results-driven advocacy in high-stakes cases.
